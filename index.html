About

Habitat for Humanity of Durham has been building, selling and repairing homes in Durham since 1985. They have built - and sold - more than 360 homes and repaired more than 400 more. The number they are most proud of is 850 - the number of Durham youth growing up in Habitat homes. These young neighbors can dream brighter futures because of the stability home brings. Habitat believes that all of our Durham neighbors deserve a safe, stable, secure place to call home. Durham is booming, and costs are rising, while incomes are not. Durham Habitat innovations like small homes and its HIP (Homeowner Incubation Program) are helping provide more affordable housing opportunities for low-income neighbors.
